Agendas and Meeting notes

Prior to the IETF 115 the Open DT has been meeting on a weekly basis. We had plans to go back to the normal working group processes, however we have found some issues that makes it preferable to continue the weekly meeting for the time being. After the IETF 115 the MPLS Network Actions (MNA) work will be discussed in Open DT meetings "on demand".
